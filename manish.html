Rohit Sharma - The Hitman

Rohit Sharma is one of the finest batsmen in the world. Known as "The Hitman," he holds the record for the highest individual score in ODI cricket (264). He is also the captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats.

Rohit has multiple double centuries in ODIs and was instrumental in India's victories in various ICC tournaments. His elegant stroke play and ability to dominate bowlers make him a fan favorite.
